Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers regarding minimum order boundaries, setup fees, shipping rates, and color configurations.

What is your minimum order limit (MOQ)?

+

For custom embroidery, our MOQ is 12 garments per logo setup. For screen printing, it is 48 garments due to mesh screen alignment setups. For Direct-to-Garment (DTG) digital printing, there is no MOQ (starts at 1 piece).

How do you calculate embroidery pricing?

+

Embroidery cost is based on stitch count (total stitches required to form the logo), garment type, and volume. A standard left chest logo averages 6,000 to 10,000 stitches.

Do you match exact corporate PMS colors?

+

Yes. For screen printing, we perform Pantone Matching System (PMS) ink blending. For embroidery, we match your vector specs to the closest solid PolyNeon thread color from our library.

What is your standard turnaround time?

+

Our standard production time is 7 to 10 business days from the moment you approve the digital stitch proof. Fast-tracked production slots (3-day turnaround) are available for urgent events.
