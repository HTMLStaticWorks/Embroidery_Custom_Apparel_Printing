Technical Specifications

Stitch Perfection

Commercial Embroidery Solutions

Our embroidery department utilizes computerized Tajima multi-head terminals. Every custom design goes through our artwork digitizing process, mapping out physical thread directions to ensure no puckering, pulls, or loose ends.

Embroidery Capabilities:

  • ✔ High density stitch support (up to 25,000 stitches per logo location)
  • ✔ 3D Puff embroidery for structural hats & jackets
  • ✔ Pantone PMS solid color matching with PolyNeon threads
  • ✔ Flame-retardant threads for industrial protective uniforms
High-Volume Runs

Premium Screen Printing Solutions

Screen printing remains the industry standard for bulk promotional items. Our automated presses lay down crisp, thick layers of ink, cured through high-temperature infrared dryers to guarantee prints that won't crack or peel for 50+ wash cycles.

Screen Print Specifications:

  • ✔ Minimum Order Limit: 48 pieces per design
  • ✔ Soft-hand plastisol and premium water-based inks
  • ✔ Up to 8 colors per design location
  • ✔ Oversized prints up to 15" x 18"
Digital Precision

DTF & DTG Digital Printing

For complex photographic logos, gradients, or quick runs, digital printing is ideal. Direct-to-Garment (DTG) prints ink directly onto cotton fibers for an ultra-soft feel, while Direct-To-Film (DTF) transfers are perfect for polyester garments.

Digital Specifications:

  • ✔ No color limits (full CMYK color range)
  • ✔ Excellent stretch resistance without design distortion
  • ✔ DTG minimum order: 1 piece | DTF minimum order: 12 pieces
  • ✔ Detailed reproduction of tiny text and micro-designs
End-to-End Fulfillment

Custom Merchandising & Supply

Our merchandising department provides complete contract packing, tagging, custom bagging, folding, and sorting services. We handle high-volume B2B supply lines with direct split-shipment logistics to multiple branches.

Merchandising Specifications:

  • ✔ Minimum Order Limit: 100 pieces per design contract
  • ✔ Relabeling, tagging, and fold/polybag setups
  • ✔ Warehouse stock holding and split-tracking delivery
  • ✔ Lightbox optical inspection and quality control assurance
