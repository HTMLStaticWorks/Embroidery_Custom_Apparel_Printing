Technical Mastery

Custom Printing Technologies

Select the optimal garment decoration technology based on your artwork detail, color count, and order size.

High-Volume Screen Printing

The gold standard for bulk orders. Utilizes heavy-duty plastisol and premium water-based inks pushed through custom stenciled mesh screens. Perfect for promotional shirts, field wear, and oversized prints.

  • ✔ Minimum Order: 48 pieces
  • ✔ Best for single to 4-color designs
  • ✔ Unmatched durability and longevity

Direct To Film (DTF) Transfers

Ideal for complex multi-colored gradients, fine line work, and small logo locations (like collars, hems, and sleeves). Prints high-res imagery onto specialized PET film, coated with adhesive powder and heat-applied.

  • ✔ Minimum Order: 12 pieces
  • ✔ Unlimited colors and gradient support
  • ✔ High stretch resistance and no cracking

Direct To Garment (DTG)

Inkjet technology printing directly onto the fabric. Excellent for ultra-soft hand feel on 100% cotton garments, photos, and short-run lifestyle branding.

  • ✔ Minimum Order: 1 piece (no setups)
  • ✔ Soft feel - inks sink into fabric fibers
  • ✔ Best on 100% organic cotton blanks

Dye Sublimation

Inks are gassified using heat to fuse directly with polyester fibers. The print becomes an absolute part of the fabric, ensuring zero texture, zero peeling, and perfect breathability for athletic jerseys.

  • ✔ Minimum Order: 24 pieces
  • ✔ 100% coverage all-over-prints possible
  • ✔ Fade-proof colors under sun and wash
