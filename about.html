About Our Company

Our Heritage

Stitching Quality Since 2012

Founded as a small custom print shop with a single-head embroidery machine, we grew by addressing a key problem in corporate apparel: the friction and delay of bulk ordering.

Today, we operate a 25,000 sq ft production facility with multi-head computer-aided stitching terminals, direct-to-garment digital presses, and automated logistics. We help thousands of businesses equip their employees and clients with branded apparel they love to wear.

Inside Our Facility

Production Workflow

A highly organized path from raw materials to final shipping is what lets us complete bulk orders with high efficiency.

Step 01 / Warehousing

Stock Sourcing & Verification

We pull high-quality blanks from local WRAP-certified warehouse inventories. Every batch undergoes strict density checks to guarantee durability before custom branding starts.

  • ✔ WRAP-Certified Garments
  • ✔ Fabric Weight Calibration
  • ✔ 100% Cotton & Blends
  • ✔ Local Warehousing Speeds
Step 02 / Design Calibration

Digitizing & Setup Calibration

Logo vectors are manually mapped to stitch-paths to prevent thread slippage. We optimize vector configurations for thread thickness and fabric texture profiles.

  • ✔ Manual Stitch Mapping
  • ✔ Density Adjustment
  • ✔ Vector Fine-Tuning
  • ✔ Preview Proof Generation
Step 03 / Multi-Head Terminals

Precision Computerized Embroidery

Garments are locked into embroidery hoops and stitched on computer-guided Tajima machines. Real-time optical sensors detect thread breaks instantly for zero-defect results.

  • ✔ Tajima Multi-Head Lines
  • ✔ Laser Positioning Alignment
  • ✔ Auto-Thread Break Recovery
  • ✔ 1000+ Stitches Per Minute
Step 04 / Quality Check & Shipping

Steam Ironing, Folding & Packing

Finished garments are steam ironed, quality-checked under specialized light panels, individually folded, and boxed for final B2B direct shipment.

  • ✔ High-Pressure Steam Ironing
  • ✔ Individual Folding & Polybags
  • ✔ Quality Light Panel Audits
  • ✔ Split-Address B2B Shipping
