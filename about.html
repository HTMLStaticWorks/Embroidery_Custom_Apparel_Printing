About Our Company

Our Heritage

Stitching Quality Since 2012

Founded as a small custom print shop with a single-head embroidery machine, we grew by addressing a key problem in corporate apparel: the friction and delay of bulk ordering.

Today, we operate a 25,000 sq ft production facility with multi-head computer-aided stitching terminals, direct-to-garment digital presses, and automated logistics. We help thousands of businesses equip their employees and clients with branded apparel they love to wear.

Our Mission

To eliminate the complexity of custom apparel ordering. We combine artisan embroidery precision with modern digital logistics to give purchasing directors, fleet managers, and marketing agencies speed, reliability, and complete control over their branded assets.

👁

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for automated B2B custom merchandise production. We aim to integrate sustainable sourcing, water-based printing inks, and recycled polyester threads to ensure corporate identity doesn't come at the cost of environmental integrity.

Inside Our Facility

Production Workflow

A highly organized path from raw materials to final shipping is what lets us complete bulk orders with high efficiency.

01

Stock Sourcing

We pull high-quality blanks from local WRAP-certified warehouse inventories.

02

Digitizing Calibration

Logo vectors are manually mapped to stitch-paths to prevent thread slippage.

03

Precision Embroidery

Garments are locked into embroidery hoops and stitched on computer-guided Tajima machines.

04

Ironing & Packing

Finished garments are steam ironed, quality-checked, folded, and boxed for shipment.
