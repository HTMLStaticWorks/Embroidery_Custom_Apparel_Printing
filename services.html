Stage 01: Setup

Select Apparel & Upload Graphics

Browse our catalog of premium polo shirts, jackets, caps, or workwear blanks. Select your desired quantities, colorways, and sizes. Then, upload your branding artwork in high-resolution vector or raster formats.

  • ✔ Over 150+ blank clothing styles stocked
  • ✔ Direct upload for AI, EPS, PDF, and PNG files
  • ✔ Automated sizing and color inventory checks
↑
Uploading logo_vector.ai (100%)
Stage 02: Calibration

Manual Digitizing & Stitch Mapping

Our professional design digitizers inspect your uploaded logos. We manually map out the vector lines into embroidery stitch paths or print separations to avoid loose threads, misalignments, or lettering blurring.

  • ✔ High-fidelity manual vectorization
  • ✔ Calibration for specific fabric backing
  • ✔ Digital color separation using Pantone guidelines
Stage 03: Validation

HD Swatch Preview & Approval

Before running the full batch, we sew a physical stitch proof on a fabric swatch. We send you a high-definition photo proof for validation. For contracts over 200 items, we mail the physical swatch directly to your office.

  • ✔ Real-world sewn preview confirmation
  • ✔ Direct check of thread sheen and thickness
  • ✔ Unlimited revisions until setup is approved
★ APPROVED
Stage 04: Production

Multi-Head Production & QC

Once approved, your order goes into production on our computer-guided Tajima embroidery lines or screen printing carousels. Every single apparel piece is individually steam-pressed, folded, polybagged, and sent with split-tracking.

  • ✔ Concurrent multi-head computerized manufacturing
  • ✔ Independent optical thread break detection
  • ✔ QC checked under high-lux light panels
