Simplified Ordering

The Bulk Order Process

How we eliminate back-and-forth communication to get your apparel produced exactly to specification.

Step 01

Select Blanks & Upload

Browse our corporate apparel catalog, select sizes, colors, and upload your brand logo files.

Step 02

Stitch Proof Verification

Our artwork team maps out the stitch lines and color schemes, sending you a high-definition proof.

Step 03

Precision Run

Once approved, our computerized multi-head machines perform the print or stitch run concurrently.

Step 04

Packaging & Tracking

Each uniform is individually steam-pressed, QA-inspected, and delivered with split-shipping tracking.
