Market Forecast

Eco-Friendly Custom Apparel Shifts

The custom apparel space is seeing a major shift toward sustainable manufacturing. Corporate buyers now actively request WRAP-certified blanks, organic ringspun cotton, and recycled polyester materials.

Furthermore, traditional chemical plastisol screen inks are being replaced by bio-degradable, water-based variations that offer a softer hand-feel and eliminate microplastic waste.

Our Sustainability Commitment

Key Industry Movements:

  • Recycled Polyester Threads: Transitioning to threads produced from ocean plastic bottles without reducing tensile strength.
  • Water-Based Ink Prints: Soft breathability that blends directly with cotton fibers.
  • On-Demand B2B Batches: Replacing bulk inventory hoarding with scheduled quarterly drop fulfillment.
