How We Work

The Embroidery Process

From digital vectorization to final high-density stitching, see how we maintain premium quality control.

Step 01

Artwork Submission

Upload your logo vector or artwork files directly. Our system handles raw vector assets and high-res source files instantly.

Step 02

Digital Proof Approval

Review a real stitch preview mock-up including exact physical thread color suggestions (Pantone-matched) before sewing starts.

Step 03

Precision Embroidery

Our computer-guided embroidery heads stitch the designs onto garments, utilizing premium poly-neon threads for wash stability.

Step 04

Quality QA & Fulfillment

Every item is individually trimmed, steam-pressed, folded, and quality checked before final secure dispatch to your office.
